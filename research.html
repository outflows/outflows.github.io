Research

In general terms, I am interested in high-energy astrophysics, with a current focus on gamma-ray bursts and blazars, particularly the following:

  • Physics of compact objects (black holes and neutron stars)
  • Magnetohydrodynamics of accretion flows and relativistic jets
  • Radiative transfer mechanisms in high-energy astrophysics
  • Shock formation, evolution and propagation
  • Electromagnetic counterparts to gravitational waves
  • High-performance computing, algorithm development
