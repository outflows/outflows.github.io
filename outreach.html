Outreach Activities

  • A primeira imagem de um buraco negro [The first image of a black hole] -- video for the outreach YouTube channel AstroTubers, explaining the significance of the M87* image to the general public. AstroTubers was created by Astronomy and Astrophysics graduate students with the aim of promoting, in an informal way, public awareness of different topics in Astronomy. It is supported by the Brazilian Astronomical Society.
  • #AstroThreadBR and #AstroMiniBR -- Twitter posts following the ongoing initiative of Brazilian astronomers and astrophysicists to spread, via Twitter, short posts and trivia aimed at the general public, which drew the attention of major newspaper O Globo.
    • Uncertainties in astronomical measurements.
    • You think the Sun is big? Think again!
    • How do we study black holes?
  • Multi-messenger astronomy: detection of gravitational waves and electromagnetic counterparts in neutron star merger -- post on The Black Hole Group's blog about the neutron star merger detected by LIGO and Virgo in 2017.
